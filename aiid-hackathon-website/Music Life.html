Let Music Understand Your Life

Music Life uses Rokid Glasses' "ego view" to capture your world in real-time, combining sensor data to recognize your state and environment, recommending perfect music for every moment. Each recommendation reflects a deep understanding of your life.

Running Companion

When you start running, the system recognizes your exercise state and tracks your step frequency, selecting rhythm-matching music from your library. Keep every step on beat, boost your workout, and unleash your energy.
